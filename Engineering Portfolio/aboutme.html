About Me:

I was born and raised in Washington D.C. where I attended Maret School from fourth grade through senior year. I then matriculated at Harvard College where I have pursued my interests spanning engineering and technology, as well as history and politics.

I have a passion for hands-on oportunities to design and fabricate solutions to challenging problems. From underwater remotely-operated vehicles, to supersonic unmanned aircraft, to CubeSats in Earth orbit, my interests span every domain, but with a particular passion for space exploration.
